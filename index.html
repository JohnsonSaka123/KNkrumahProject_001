Dr. Kwame Nkrumah

A Ghanaian politician, political theorist, and revolutionary leader.

About the Legend

👉 Dr. Kwame Nkrumah was born on Tuesday, 21st September 1909, in Nkroful, Gold Coast (now Ghana).

👉 He served as Prime Minister of the Gold Coast from 1952 until 1957, when it gained independence from Britain.

👉 He had his elementary education at a Roman Catholic elementary school near Half Assini and later attended Achimota College in 1930.

👉 He started his career as a teacher at Roman Catholic junior schools in Elmina and Axim and at a seminary.

👉 He pursued further studies in the United States, where he attended Lincoln University and obtained a master's degree.

👉 After leaving the United States in May 1945, he went to England, where he organized the 5th Pan-African Congress in Manchester.

👉 Kwame Nkrumah played a pivotal role in the struggle for Ghana's independence from British colonial rule and became the country's first Prime Minister and President when it gained independence in 1957.
